Vegan Chocolate Cake Recipe

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Granulated sugar
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Baking powder
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Almond milk
  • Vegetable oil
  • Vanilla extract
  • Hot water

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the almond milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract.
